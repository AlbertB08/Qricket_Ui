Yes. You can upgrade to VIP or Exhibitor as long as slots are available. Bring your original QR ticket at the registration desk.
Is parking included with my pass? Open Close
General Admission includes spectator parking. VIP and Exhibitor passes have priority parking zones near the main entrance.
Do I need to print my ticket? Open Close
No. We accept mobile QR codes at all gates. Make sure your phone is charged for scanning.
